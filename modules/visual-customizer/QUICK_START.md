## ğŸ¨ Common Customizations

### Change Card Colors

1. Click **Visual** mode
2. Open **Colors** section
3. Click color swatch for:
   - **Card Header** - Top bar color
   - **Card Background** - Main card color
   - **Button Color** - Website button

### Adjust Spacing

1. Open **Spacing** section
2. Drag sliders:
   - **Card Padding** - Inside space
   - **Grid Gap** - Space between cards
   - **Border Radius** - Rounded corners

### Add Shadows & Effects

1. Open **Effects** section
2. Choose from dropdowns:
   - **Box Shadow** - None to Heavy
   - **Hover Effect** - Lift, Scale, or Shadow
   - **Transition Speed** - How fast changes happen
